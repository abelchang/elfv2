Who Are We?

The Envision Life Foundation is a global NGO dedicated to empowering children in underprivi-leged areas around the globe, and provide them with accessible education and opportunities to connect with the world.

What Are We Doing?

We believe that all children deserve a chance to freely explore their identity, meaning, and pur-pose in life.

A complex issue needs a collaborative solution.

To achieve that goal, we believe the answer lies in charity 2.0—the building of a diverse charita-ble ecosystem of people, organizations, and institutions across disciplines, nationalities, and cul-tures, and most important of all, of the community we seek to help.

Our lives are guided by people we meet, and relationships we build.

We aim to ignite children’s passion to think, to dream, and to imagine by connecting children with their community, with their culture, with the land they live on, and with rest of the world.

What is ELF Glocal Initiative?

To achieve our mission, we have devised a series of programmatic interventions known as the ELF Glocal Initiative.

Initiative:
Community Dawn

In Community Dawn, we, community, and local experts work collaboratively to make the community a better place.

ELF

Our role is to plan with and work alongside experts and community to build an environment where children can feel free to explore, to connect, to imagine, to thrive.

Community

Our aim is to first invite few motivated persons, and gradually involve the entire community, to take lead in facing and overcoming social challenges one step at a time.

Experts

We believe that, by building a network of local experts, we can provide a more comprehensive assessment, services, and guidance to the community.
